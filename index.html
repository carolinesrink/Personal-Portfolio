About Me


I am a Front End Web Developer with a background in Marketing and a Certification in Full Stack Web Development through the Georgia Institute of Technology's Web Development Program. Throughout the course, I worked alongside classmates in a team setting and delivered award winning projects with user friendly designs. I served as Front End Lead on all three projects throughout the program and offered creative solutions for problem solving.

I was born and raised in Atlanta, GA. I attended the University of West Georgia for my undergraduate degree. My favorite hobby is traveling and I have been to eight countries so far. I participated in two study abroad programs while I was completing my undergraduate degree.

I also have two cats, Stella and Goose, who are always worth a mention.
